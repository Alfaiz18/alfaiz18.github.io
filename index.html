How I Work

A clear process, start to finish.

The same four-stage approach on every WordPress or Shopify build, whether it's a small storefront or a full custom platform.

01

Discover & Plan

Understand the brief, the business goal, and the technical constraints before writing a single line of code.

02

Design & Architect

Plan the theme/data architecture — metafields, metaobjects, ACF fields, or CPTs — so the client team can manage content independently later.

03

Build & Integrate

Develop with performance and SEO in mind from the start, integrating any third-party tools, APIs, or automations the project needs.

04

Launch & Support

QA across devices, ship it, and stay available for fixes, iteration, or the next phase of the project.
